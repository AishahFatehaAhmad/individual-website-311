WELCOME TO MY WEBSITE

I have never had many friends in my whole life. In all my 20 years of living on earth, I only have 3 best friends. Due to my introvertness in daily life, I hardly have friends. Due to my introvertness, I have low self-confidence and it constantly makes me feel that people are judging me, whether it is about my clothes, my appearance, or other issues. Therefore, I tend to block other people from being close to me or from being in my world because I am scared that they will get bored with me and then leave me or betray me. You can probably guess that I've been left and betrayed in the past.Yeah, it's true that I got left and betrayed by people that I thought were my friends. I even got judged and shamed because of my physical appearance and got called names too. But as I grew up, I tend to let things like this go away. I keep reminding myself that Allah has created me perfectly and I should be thankful for it. Therefore, whenever it happens again, I also remind myself that I should improve myself and ignore negative comments from toxic people in my life. All I can say is, I know it's sad and hurtful when people say bad things about you when you did nothing wrong, but believe me, if you just ignore their comments and negativity, you will be happier. A gentle reminder from me is to not become like them to other people, even though you don't like them. If you don't like someone, just don't meet them or talk to them, but if you have to, then just talk to them about important things such as work, your studies, and other things.
